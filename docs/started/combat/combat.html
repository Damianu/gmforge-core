Rolling/Setting Initiative

You can manage initiatives by right clicking on them in the asset list, combat-> Roll/Set Initiative.
You can assign the initiative by setting it directly, or entering a macro formula.
If you enter nothing, it will roll the default initiative equation


'Hidden' Combattants

Hidden combattants only appear to those who have 'Visible' rights to them. If the current turn is the same as a hidden combattants, they will appear in the initiative order, but with a hidden image/name.

You can add hidden combattants after a combat a started, so plan accordingly.

You can hide combattants by right clicking on them, then navigating to combat->hide (or unhide).
